April 15, 2025

SCMMA Fighter Zach Freeman Secures Major Victory

This past weekend, SCMMA's own Zach Freeman secured a spectacular victory at the regional MMA championship, submitting his opponent in the second round with a perfectly executed rear-naked choke. This win marks Freeman's fifth consecutive victory and puts him in contention for a title shot later this year.

"The training at SCMMA prepared me perfectly for this fight," Freeman said after his victory. "Coach Mike's game plan was spot on, and the entire team's support was incredible. I couldn't have done it without them."

New Advanced BJJ Program Launching in May

SCMMA is excited to announce the launch of our new Advanced Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program starting May 1st. This specialized program is designed for experienced practitioners looking to refine their techniques and prepare for high-level competition.

The program will be led by Coach Mike Rogers, a 3rd-degree black belt under Rodrigo Vaghi, and will focus on advanced submissions, transitions, and competition strategies. Classes will be held on Tuesday and Thursday evenings from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM.

March 20, 2025

Summer Martial Arts Camp for Kids Registration Now Open

Registration is now open for SCMMA's annual Summer Martial Arts Camp for kids ages 7-14. The camp will run for six weeks from June 15th to July 24th, with both full-day and half-day options available.

Campers will learn martial arts techniques, participate in fun games and activities, and develop important life skills such as discipline, respect, and confidence. Each week features a different theme, with special guest instructors and exciting field trips planned throughout the summer.

Early bird registration discounts are available until April 30th, and spaces are limited, so sign up soon to secure your child's spot!

March 10, 2025

Special Seminar with UFC Champion Announced

SCMMA is thrilled to announce an upcoming special seminar with UFC Champion and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt Marcus Silva on April 22nd. This exclusive event will provide attendees with the opportunity to learn techniques and strategies from one of the sport's elite competitors.

The seminar will cover both striking and grappling techniques, with a focus on effective combinations for MMA competition. Participants will also have the chance to ask questions, take photos, and get autographs during a meet-and-greet session following the technical portion of the seminar.

Tickets are available now, with special pricing for SCMMA members. Don't miss this rare opportunity to train with a world champion!

February 28, 2025

SCMMA Upgrades Facility with New Equipment

SCMMA has recently completed a major upgrade to our training facility, adding new equipment and expanding our mat space to better serve our growing membership. The improvements include new heavy bags, a dedicated strength and conditioning area, and an additional 1,000 square feet of mat space.

"We're committed to providing our members with the best possible training environment," said SCMMA owner Mike Rogers. "These upgrades will allow us to offer more classes, accommodate more students, and continue to deliver the high-quality instruction that SCMMA is known for."

The expanded facility will officially open on March 5th with a special open house event featuring demonstrations, free trial classes, and membership specials.
