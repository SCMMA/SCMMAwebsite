About Our Gym

SCMMA is a no-nonsense, old-school gym built for real training. Whether you're sharpening your skills or pushing your limits, our facility has everything you need to get to work.

Our Facilities

Training Areas

Our gym features multiple training areas to accommodate different classes and training styles:

  • Strength & Conditioning Room: Packed with weights, squat racks, and cardio machines, this space is all about building power, endurance, and grit.
  • Jiu-Jitsu Room: With fully padded floors and walls, this setup is perfect for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and grappling, keeping training intense and safe.
  • Striking Room: Built for boxing, kickboxing, and MMA, this space features heavy bags, padded flooring, and a boxing ring for sparring and technique development.

Equipment

We provide a comprehensive range of equipment to support all aspects of martial arts training:

  • Heavy bags of various weights and styles
  • Thai pads, focus mitts, and striking shields
  • Grappling dummies and training aids
  • Strength and conditioning equipment
  • Mobility and recovery tools

Amenities

Our facility includes several amenities to enhance your training experience:

  • Men's and women's locker rooms
  • Viewing area for parents and spectators
  • Water station and rest rooms
  • WiFi throughout the facility
